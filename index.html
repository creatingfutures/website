Our Story

Skilled manpower is one of the most vital aspects in today’s competitive world. Organizations are in constant need for trained and skilled workforce to address its growing demands. The current education system being academically-oriented, do not focus on the skills required to perform efficiently in a corporate environment. In today’s scenario, lakhs of students graduate each year and with the expected economic growth, the challenge of having a skilled workforce will multiply, since more and more jobs are expected to be skill-based jobs. Given the increased numbers of graduates and increase in skill-based jobs, there is an urgent need to address this. It is the students from the rural areas and many from the urban areas that are most affected, due to their lack of exposure to the enhanced skills. And in this scenario, thousands do not get employment leading to a cycle of poverty

Creating Futures is Born

Given this urgent need for capability development and to tackle unemployment, the idea of Creating Futures was born. We believe that education empowers us. It gives us the necessary skills and makes us capable enough to earn a living! In today’s world there is no dearth of educated people. But, there is a dearth is of “skilled” educated individuals. What’s important in today’s world is how you represent Yourself!

We believe that we can make a difference in building the required skills that will enable the beneficiaries in getting that great job. We believe that we can provide the necessary tools that will make them successful. To achieve this objective, we at Creating Futures have set up this non-profit and non-government organization driven by passionate individuals.

Objectives of Creating Futures

Creating Futures will work towards the achievement of these through two complementary programs:

  • Value-Addition Program
    This is a short-duration (approximately, 10 days) program, and focuses on honing skills, predominantly soft skills, that will enable youth find employment, and aimed at educated youth, with minimum qualification of 12th standard.

  • Practical, Real-Life Experience Program
    This is a long-duration (approximately, 6-9 months) program, with primary focus on internship, and aimed at skills needed for the IT industry.

Both these programs are aimed at the educated rural and urban youth from economically weak background.

Internship Program

Our Internship program prepares college students and young professionals become well-rounded individuals armed with the required experience and expertise in their field of work.

The program participants work on real-time projects. Necessary trainings in technology, processes and soft skills and continued interactions with professionals in their area of work, is what we offer. Each participant has a well-defined development path supported by experienced professionals. Mentors are assigned too..

The program is for a duration of three months after which job placement opportunities will be provided.

E2E Program

Education to Employability Program

In the job market, having an edge over others, makes all the difference. Communication skills, presentation skills, interview skills and business etiquette are some of the pivotal skills that can make some students stand apart from the rest and land the golden opportunities.

A personal effectiveness program enhancing such skills and creating corporate awareness will ensure better preparedness while attending interviews and also later in their professional career. Our Education to Employability is such a program.

Our objective is to empower college students with tools for enhancing their personal and professional effectiveness thus leading to improved employability and professional excellence.

Spoken English

In today’s competitive job environment, it is essential that the young professionals have a good command over the English language. The objective is to instill in the students the confidence to be able to converse, convey and communicate in English

The program covers aspects like Word building, Pronunciation, Basic grammar, Simple Sentences and Social Conversation

Life skills Program

Life Skills are abilities for adaptive and positive behavior that enables individuals to deal effectively with the demands and challenges of everyday life - (WHO).

Life Skills are competencies that help students and young professionals function well in their social, private and public environments. Our program helps in personality development and anchors the objective of overall grooming of students. Our methodology involves building these essential skills sequentially, with small steps and they are then acquired through “learn-by-doing” activities.

Life Skills programs are designed specifically by taking into consideration the student's background, circumstances, age and developmental stage.

Career Counselling & Guidance

The aim of this program is to introduce the youth to the multitude of career options available for them in today’s world and guide them in choosing an option that best suits them. Focusing on creating self-awareness – thus enabling the students in discovering their innate qualities, abilities and skills, we guide them in aligning their interests and skills to their current circumstances and using this as the anchor, they can plot their career options.

This program also looks at Career Counselling.

Teacher Development

The essence of a teacher lies in the ability to uncover a student’s true potential and inspire them. Through this program we involve the teachers in activities that aim to reinforce the pivotal role that they play in a child’s life. We sensitize them to the opportunity that they have in influencing a life and through that the society at large. We work with the teachers in expanding their perspectives, devising ways to be more responsive to the needs and adapt to the potential of individual students. This we believe makes a child’s learning engagement impactful.

Computer Skills

We work with students who do not have the privilege of accessing computers and hence we have designed this programme to equip them with the knowledge of basic computer usage. This structure of this programme facilitates exposure to simple computer operations, email usage and furthermore, attempts to introduce them to the world of MS- Word, Excel & Powerpoint.

Emotional Management

Many of our students endure difficult relationships and circumstances in their lives. To face their day to day life, they need to be capable of managing their emotions and responding appropriately.We start with helping them identify their emotions and recognize how they currently respond to those emotions. Using techniques/tools like Group Therapy, Empathy, Conflict Management and others, we guide them on means to reduce stress and transform the way they can respond to life. This helps them in positively engaging in their life.

Wellbeing Programmes

This is our program for uneducated youth and school drop outs. Our endeavor is to work with them to cultivate the thought that despite their difficult situations in life, they can aspire, achieve and accomplish their desires. Our workshops transform into safe havens for them to express themselves without fear. Through our activities we make them feel worthy, appreciated and safe. Our ultimate aim is to germinate a genuine desire for improvement.

Soft skills

Mastery over Soft Skills is quintessential in making a mark in any career. It is essential for anyone entering the corporate world. Leadership skills, Teamwork, Effective Communication, Negotiation, Business Etiquette and others are among the soft skills that we have in our program bouquet. With this program we reach out to students who are in the final year of their studies or have just completed their education.

Whole School development

The Creating Futures’ Whole School Development program is focused on the concept of ‘sustainability’. Starting early in the lifecycle, we target the educational institutions through whom the young adults start their learning journeys. We look at the holistic development of the school with the school management, teachers, parents and other community members collaborating to lay the foundation for a wholesome educational upbringing of the students.

Personality Development

Living responsibly and Self Responsibility is the key driver.

This is a program with a difference. We set the students on a path of exploration, encouraging them to look within and map their journey of life. In the process, they stumble upon their obstacles, appreciate their achievements and while doing so, identify their unique strengths and capabilities that have helped them sustain and survive. This insightful information about themselves enables them to make better decisions. Thus enabling them in living life responsibly and purposefully
